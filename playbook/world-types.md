## Hard vs soft dial

| Dial | Meaning | Doc implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hard | Explicit rules, maps, timelines | Fill reality rules and timeline fully; leave few gaps |
| Soft | Atmospheric gaps, ruins, implication | Mark `# Intentional Gaps` in lore; forbid contradicting gaps later |

Record dial in governance World Profile. Changing dial mid-build is a MINOR or MAJOR canon event depending on breakage.
