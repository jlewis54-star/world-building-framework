# 41 Brand Guidelines

## Public Positioning (safe)

Harbor Line publishes freight folklore as print objects and radio episodes from a fictional North Atlantic port. Partners receive marks, palette, type, and three material bans. Founding myth details beyond one-line positioning stay internal.

## Marks

| Mark | Clear space | Min size | Colorways | Forbidden |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Primary logotype | 1 x cap height | 24 px digital / 15 mm print | Black, red on warm white | Gradients, shadows |

## Typography (partner subset)

IBM Plex Sans (body), IBM Plex Mono (forms). See `22_visual_core` for weights. No substitute fonts.

## Color (partner subset)

Black #1A1A1A, accent red #C41E24 on warm white stock #F4F1EA. No hyperlink blue.

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Show stamped tags and forms | Add faux rust or distress filters |
| Cite run sizes and dates | Promise delivery times |

## Merchandise Materials

| Category | Allowed | Banned |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Apparel blanks | Heavyweight cotton, black/natural | Polyester performance tees |
| Print methods | Letterpress, single-spot offset | Foil unless approved |
| Packaging | Kraft mailers, uncoated inserts | Gloss boxes |

## Approval Workflow

1. Submit: PDF proof + material spec sheet.
2. Reviewers: brand ops + visual lead.
3. SLA: 3 business days.
4. Rejection criteria: fail Shipping Ledger check or banned lexicon terms on packaging.

## Lore Disclosure Policy

| May appear publicly | Must stay internal |
| --- | --- |
| Pier 9 incident one-liner | Unresolved fraud vs error debate |
| Tag run numbers and dates | Night Dispatcher identity |
| Radio episode titles | Full pier ledger excerpts |
