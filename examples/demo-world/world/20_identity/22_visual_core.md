## Typography System

| Role | Family | Weight | Tracking / size notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Display | IBM Plex Sans | 600 | Tight tracking on headlines only |
| Body | IBM Plex Sans | 400 | 11/16 pt print minimum |
| Mono / system | IBM Plex Mono | 400 | Forms, manifests, timestamps |

Rules: No serif display. No geometric sans outside this table.

## Identity Marks

| Mark | Allowed uses | Forbidden uses |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary logotype | Print, radio art, tags | On food packaging |
| Sub-mark (HL monogram in rectangle) | Stamps, favicons | Stretch, outline effects |

## Silhouette / Shape Language

Institutions use rectangles and hard corners. Care objects (mugs, meal tins) may use 4 px corner radius max. No circles as logo containers.
