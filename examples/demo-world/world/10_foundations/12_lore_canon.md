Harbor Line sits in a recognizable North Atlantic port city where freight, radio, and print shops overlap. Events are plausible; emphasis is on clerical myth-making around real infrastructure.

## The Rules of Reality

1. No supernatural cargo. Anomalies are clerical, not magical.
2. Institutions speak in rectangles (forms, ledgers, terminals); personal artifacts may use rounded corners.
3. Public-facing timelines stop at documented artifacts; oral stories cite a source or stay internal.

## Intentional Gaps (soft dial)

- Exact pier numbering before 1970 stays unspecified.
- Whether the 1974 incident was fraud or error is unresolved; both readings are valid in fiction.
