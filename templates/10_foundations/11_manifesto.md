## Example (studio density)

> **Friction:** Preservation vs. disposable speed.  
> **Emotion:** Solitude.  
> **Myth:** A coastal archive that refused digitization until the last physical ledger burned. The world begins at the decision to rebuild in matter, not cloud.  
> **Beliefs:** (1) Surfaces must show wear. (2) Speed is not a virtue. (3) Silence outranks explanation. (4) Tools outlive campaigns. (5) No pastel optimism.  
> **Enemy:** SaaS-smooth interfaces, stock lifestyle photography, motivational copy.
